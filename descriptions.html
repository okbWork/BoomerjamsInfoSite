Product Descriptions

This page provides plain-language descriptions for Boomerjams applications.

Festiv

Festiv is designed to help users discover local events ranging from nightlife and concerts to campus and community happenings. The goal is to reduce the effort needed to find quality events and make social planning easier.

Pairus

Pairus is a dating platform in progress, centered on compatibility and healthy communication. It aims to improve connection quality by supporting thoughtful matching and more intentional interactions.
