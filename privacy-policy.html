Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Boomerjams values privacy and is committed to being transparent about data usage. This policy outlines the baseline approach across Boomerjams portfolio products, including pre-release apps such as Festiv and Pairus.

Information Collected

  • Contact information submitted through direct communication.
  • Basic usage and diagnostic data to improve app reliability.
  • Optional profile details where users choose to provide them.

How Information Is Used

  • To operate and improve app functionality.
  • To respond to support requests and inquiries.
  • To maintain safety, integrity, and abuse prevention.

Data Sharing

Boomerjams does not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with service providers that support app infrastructure, or when required by law.
